  • What are your rights? Depending on where you are located geographically, the applicable privacy law may mean you have certain rights regarding your personal information.
  • How do you exercise your rights? The easiest way to exercise your rights is by submitting a data subject access request, or by contacting us.

17. CONTACTS PERMISSIONS

In Short: We do not request broad access to your device contacts. Where contact selection is needed, we use the Android Contact Picker.

In compliance with Google Play's Contacts Permissions policy, the App does not request READ_CONTACTS, WRITE_CONTACTS, or GET_ACCOUNTS permissions for broad access to your contact list. Where a feature requires selecting a contact (e.g., inviting a friend to play), we exclusively use the Android Contact Picker — a secure, system-managed interface that lets you choose a single contact without granting the App access to your full address book. This minimizes data collection and protects your privacy.

18. LOCATION PERMISSIONS

In Short: Precise location is requested only via the Android location button, using the minimum scope necessary, and only for the specific feature you activate.

In compliance with Google Play's updated Location Permissions policy, if the App requests precise location access, it does so using the Android location button as the recommended minimum scope. Location access is requested only in the foreground, only when a user-initiated feature requires it, and only for the duration of that feature's use.

We do not use geofencing as a foreground service use case. If geofencing functionality is offered, it is implemented via the Android Geofence API, which does not require a foreground service permission. You may revoke location access at any time in your device's app settings.

19. PHOTO & VIDEO PERMISSIONS

In Short: We only access photos or videos when you explicitly trigger a feature that requires it. We never access your media library broadly.

In compliance with Google Play's Photo and Video Permissions policy, the App requests access to photos or videos only when a specific, user-initiated action requires it — for example, uploading a profile picture. We use the Android photo picker where applicable, which gives you precise control over which files are shared. We do not access, store, or share your media files beyond the scope of the action you have requested.

20. ACCOUNT TRANSFER POLICY

In Short: Any transfer of developer account ownership follows Google Play's official workflow exclusively.

In compliance with Google Play's Account Transfer policy, any transfer of our developer account or its associated apps is conducted exclusively through the "Transfer ownership" workflow available within the Google Play Console. We do not use unofficial methods to transfer account ownership. This ensures continuity of policy compliance and user data protections across any ownership change.

21. AGE-RESTRICTED CONTENT & FUNCTIONALITY

In Short: Content or features restricted to adults are protected by age-gating to prevent access by minors.

Quetiemals is a game intended for general audiences. To the extent the App incorporates any features that may qualify as age-restricted under Google Play's Age-Restricted Content and Functionality policy (such as social or matchmaking features that are incidental to core gameplay), we implement effective age-gating mechanisms to prevent minors from accessing those features.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately at support@teqvault.online so we can promptly delete it.
