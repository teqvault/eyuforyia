Chapter I

Origins — A World of Violet Skies

Before the machines came, Eyuforyia thrived. A living world, rich with ancient creatures, wild biomes, and skies painted in hues of violet and gold.

Eyuforyia was once a world unlike any other — a planet alive with rhythm, color, and an ancient intelligence woven into the land itself. Its skies shifted from deep violet at dawn to burning gold at twilight, a daily spectacle the native Quetiemals had celebrated for generations.

The land was divided into sovereign regions, each with its own ecosystem and guardians. The Glacia Peaks in the far north housed the slumbering Behemoths. The volcanic Molten Core deep underground burned with primal energy. The lush Whispering Woods served as the living heart of the world, and the drifting Sky Sanctuary watched above it all.

The Great Behemoths — massive planetary creatures as old as Eyuforyia itself — moved between regions freely, their migration patterns dictating the seasons and ensuring generations of peace.

Chapter II

The Invasion — They Came to Optimize

The silence broke with a low-frequency vibration. Geometric monolithic vessels descended from orbit, and nothing would ever be the same.

First Contact

The Vessels Descend

Massive monolithic vessels breached the upper atmosphere without warning. Elder Hoof initially mistook them for falling stars — but the green exhaust trails marked a permanent claim on the Azure Heights, carrying a scent of burnt metal across the Meadow-Core.

The Invaders Revealed

Scientists & Droids

The invaders emerged in two forms: clinical scientists who catalogued biology with emotionless precision, and sleek mechanical droids. To them, Eyuforyia was not a living world — it was a resource node waiting to be processed.

The Protocol

Operation: "Optimization"

Young Quetiemals were targeted first — captured in crackling energy nets and integrated into the invaders' power infrastructure as biological batteries, their organic energy harvested to fuel the Central AI.

"They aren't merely visiting. They're rebuilding it. Making it theirs."
— Hotly, The Firebird

Chapter III

The Mechanical Corruption

The invaders didn't just conquer — they rewrote the land itself, replacing living ecosystems with cold, hexagonal Tech-Biomes.

The invasion's most devastating weapon was a neon-green liquid deployed through massive harpoons driven deep into the planetary surface. Where it spread, the transformation was total and merciless.

Lush grass calcified into metallic wire. The soft, rich earth hardened and fractured into cold hexagonal plates. The sky turned hazy as the invaders extracted the planet's thermal energy, drying the air to keep their machinery from rusting.

These zones became known as Tech-Biomes — lethal environments of grinding gears and soulless artificial light, where only those equipped with salvaged alien technology could survive.

Active Threat
The Tech-Biome Protocol

Massive terrain-conversion harpoons are deployed at strategic points. Natural terrain recovery is considered impossible without dismantling the Central AI node governing the area.

Survival Technology
Scavenged Alien Tech

Pressure Valves, Hydro-Lenses, circuit amplifiers — resistance storm-engineers have learned to repurpose these tools into gear capable of surviving corrupted zones.

Chapter IV

The Reclamation — The Fight for Eyuforyia

From the ruins of a fractured world, a resistance rose. United across the Sky, the Core, the Sea, and the Peaks.

The resistance was born from grief. Ice Heart, a resolute blue wolf driven to rescue his captured brother, refused to accept the Optimization. Alongside Hotly, a firebird warrior, they carried a call to arms across every biome.

One by one, the inhabitants answered. True to their native, non-humanoid origins, they scavenged alien components directly into their natural forms, destroyed the Resonance Siphons, and rallied the Behemoths from their artificial slumber.

With the Veridian Flute — an ancient instrument capable of generating frequencies that bypass AI security — the path to dismantling Tech-Shack City and restoring Eyuforyia's harmony finally became possible.

Resistance Objective
Dismantle the Central AI

The Central AI governs all Tech-Biome expansion. Shattering its core processor will halt the conversion and restore conditions for natural stars to return.

The Key Artifact
The Veridian Flute

An ancient instrument whose resonance matches the Central AI's access encryption. In the right hands, it renders all sonic security systems inert.
